WhatsApp Analyzer

importance of messaging apps in our lives cannot be overstated; we share essentially our whole lives on WhatsApp.
What if we could get a more visual perspective of our chats?

The analyzer cleans up your data and then creates graphs which show most sent messages by each user, how many messages sent on a monthly basis, word clouds, etc. To see all of the different graphs and more, check the github repo!
